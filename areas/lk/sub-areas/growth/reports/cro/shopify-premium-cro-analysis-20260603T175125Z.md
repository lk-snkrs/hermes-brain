## CRO diagnosis

LK already has strong premium assets:

- boutique premium / Jardins positioning;
- physical store proof;
- 100% authentic/original positioning;
- 10x without interest;
- free shipping threshold;
- WhatsApp support;
- rich editorial product descriptions;
- size/fit guidance;
- collection SEO/GEO work;
- PDP Curadoria LK / variation thumbnail rollout.

Main CRO issue is not lack of content. It is friction and confidence packaging:

1. Premium trust is present but dispersed. PDPs mention authenticity, store, reviews, shipping, returns, prazo and WhatsApp, but the conversion zone should package this more cleanly around the buy decision.
2. Product availability/prazo creates anxiety. Premium buyers need exact clarity: in stock vs sob encomenda, when ships, what happens after purchase, and whether WhatsApp confirmation is optional or required.
3. Some policy messaging is inconsistent: examples include 7-day exchange in some visible trust areas vs 30-day return/exchange in LLM/policy-style content; some PDP descriptions mention both 7 and 30 days.
4. Premium positioning should avoid cheap CRO patterns: countdowns, fake urgency, discount spam, oversized badges, noisy trust seals. LK should use concierge confidence, provenance, physical-store authority, and fit assurance.
5. Collection pages have huge assortments. With 1,158 sneakers, discovery/filtering and top-slot merchandising matter as much as PDP optimization.
6. SERP evidence shows high consumer anxiety around original vs premium/replica. For "comprar tênis original premium brasil", Google mixes marketplaces, replica/linha premium pages, local pack, PAA about original vs linha premium. LK must explicitly separate "premium boutique" from "linha premium/primeira linha" ambiguity.
7. For "onitsuka tiger mexico 66 original brasil", LK is already ranking #1 organic in the sampled mobile SERP. CRO gains here should prioritize converting high-intent SEO traffic.

## Priority opportunities

### P0 — Above-the-fold PDP confidence block

Add or refine a compact decision-support block near price/CTA:

- 100% original + nota fiscal
- Loja física Jardins/SP
- Envio: in stock ships up to 2 business days OR sob encomenda 4–6 weeks
- Troca/devolução clear and consistent
- WhatsApp concierge: confirm size/prazo before buying

Tone: premium, quiet, not badge-heavy.

### P0 — Availability/prazo clarity

Create clear states:

- Em estoque: "Pronto para envio · até 2 dias úteis"
- Sob encomenda: "Compra assistida · prazo estimado 4–6 semanas"
- Esgotado: waitlist + WhatsApp sourcing request

Avoid a situation where buyer is unsure if add-to-cart is immediate purchase, reservation, or concierge order.

### P0 — Fix policy consistency

Audit and normalize:

- 7 days vs 30 days wording;
- exchange vs return vs right of withdrawal;
- PDP, footer, cart, checkout-adjacent copy, llms page, collection trust strips.

### P1 — Premium proof stack on PDP

Instead of generic trust badges, use LK-specific proof:

- photo/link to Jardins flagship;
- CNPJ/address available but not visually heavy;
- reviews count/rating near title/CTA when available;
- "atendimento humano para tamanho e disponibilidade";
- original box/tags/NF packaging proof;
- optional product-specific provenance notes for high-ticket collabs.

### P1 — Size confidence module

Fit uncertainty is a major sneaker friction.

Improve by model family:

- "Como veste" summarized in 2 lines near size selector;
- compare to Nike AF1 / Samba / NB / Jordan;
- feet-wide note;
- WhatsApp fit help link with prefilled product/size text;
- keep full table below.

### P1 — Cart drawer reassurance

Cart should answer the last objections without pressure:

- delivery estimate / shipping threshold;
- returns/exchange summary;
- authenticity/original + NF;
- WhatsApp help link;
- payment/installment reminder.

No countdown timer recommended for LK premium.

### P1 — Collection discovery for 1,158 items

For broad collection pages, test:

- curated quick filters: Novidades, Mais procurados, Até R$ X, Pronta entrega, Feminino/masculino/unissex, Onitsuka, New Balance, Samba/Gazelle, Travis/Jordan;
- sticky sort/filter on mobile;
- best-seller/new top slots already need business-rule validation;
- preserve premium editorial intro but keep product grid high on mobile.

### P2 — Search/SXO landing pages

Build/strengthen pages around SERP anxieties:

- "Tênis original vs linha premium: como comprar com segurança"
- "Onde comprar Onitsuka Tiger original no Brasil"
- "Guia de tamanho Onitsuka Tiger / New Balance / Jordan"
- Collection/PDP CTAs from these guides to products and WhatsApp.

### P2 — Review/media system

Where possible:

- bring review summary above fold;
- highlight reviews with customer photos if available;
- ask post-purchase for fit/size feedback;
- use UGC carefully; premium aesthetic matters.

### P2 — Assisted-buying funnel

For high-ticket/sob encomenda products:

- primary CTA can remain buy/add where appropriate;
- secondary CTA: "Confirmar tamanho e prazo";
- prefilled WhatsApp with product handle, selected size, price/prazo;
- track assisted conversion separately.
